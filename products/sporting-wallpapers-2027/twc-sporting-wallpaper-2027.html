How to Set Your Wallpaper

  1. Select your device from the dropdown above.
  2. Click Download Wallpaper to save the PNG.
  3. iPhone / iPad: Open the image in Photos, tap the share icon, then Use as Wallpaper.
  4. Android: Open the image in Gallery, tap the three-dot menu, then Set as wallpaper.
  5. Choose Lock Screen, Home Screen, or both.
