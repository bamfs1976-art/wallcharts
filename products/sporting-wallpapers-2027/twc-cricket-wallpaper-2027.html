How to Set Your Wallpaper

  1. Select your device from the dropdown above.
  2. Click Download PNG to save the image.
  3. iPhone / iPad: Settings → Wallpaper → Add New → choose the downloaded image.
  4. Android: Long-press home screen → Wallpaper → choose the downloaded image.
  5. Set as Lock Screen for best results.
